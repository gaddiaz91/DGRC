About

I was born and raised in Chicago and have lived on every side of Chicago, Illinois. I reside on the Northside specifically in the Rodgers Park Edgewater community where I attend a program called Year up. Year-Up Chicago is a leading one-year career development program with more than 250 corporate partners around the country; the program consists of an accelerated college-level curriculum, professional training and Networking, and concludes with a six-month formal internship program.

Currently, I am a class 11 student at Year Up Chicago with a track in Hardware with a focus in cyber security, while, working on my skills to be a strong security expert here at Year Up. I will do this by acquiring my Nanodegree, CompTIA A+, Network+, and Security+ certifications by spring 2016. These are the skills necessary to put me on track while paving the way to my success. Outside from the program I represent a number of companies as a brand ambassador, by being a brand ambassador I have learned how to network, promote and educate people.

Overall, I have the desire to learn and increase my professional development in self-help. My hobbies are simply hanging out with friends and people of different backgrounds, in addition, reading personal empowerment and leadership books but most importantly I enjoy the company of people because each person brings something new to the table that we can all learn from. A strategy that I use to be successful is repetitive positive affirmation. Repetitive positive affirmation assists with manifesting my desires, as every time I repeat an affirmation, the conscious mind plants the seed, and the subconscious mind waters it.
