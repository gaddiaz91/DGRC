About

Hello everyone, my name is Giuseppina. I was born in Providence, Rhode Island, but I was raised in Chicago and have lived here my whole life. I love my city!

A little bit about me: I am a highly motivated individual, interested in progressing my knowledge and skills in programming. Currently, I am focused on pursuing a nanodegree in programming through Udacity. I also have plans to pursue MTA Database certification through Microsoft.

Along with my interest in programming, I love to read books and collect different types of coins. My passion for books stems from the young age of five when I taught myself to read. In addition to collecting books, I maintain my own personal library and catalog system as I also enjoy organizing and maintaining order. Furthermore, I delight in finding and collecting various types of American and European coins. I especially enjoy antiques, the older the better, as I find some older ones to be intriguingly elaborate or plain.
