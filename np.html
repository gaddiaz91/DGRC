Hey Guys!
Below I have some tips for you when writing. Never underestimate the power of communication, take your grammar and punctuation seriously. Take it as a word to the wise, knowing how to communicate effectively is the difference between you being taken seriously and being seen as lacking in ability. Remember to always, always, read and re-read over everything you write.

I can’t stress how important it is to always double even triple check what you’ve written. Any documents you write, once put out there, are concrete, there is no taking it back. It is unfortunate but very true that first impressions are lasting, they can make or break you. Always put your best foot forward and take the time to read over, out loud, what you’ve written.

As I’ve stated before, never underestimate the power of communication, knowing how to communicate effectively is the difference between you getting the job and you being unemployed. Take your writing seriously, to many people in whatever position you eventually end up in it will become what you are known by. Be respectful, straight forward, and take the time to make sure your grammar and punctuation are correct.
Best wishes,
Giuseppina








Writing TIPS!

Do NOT Use Short Hand/Slang! – Always be mindful of your audience. Nothing says unprofessional like using slang. In a professional setting spell out all the words. Do not use any sort of slang! Save the slang for your friends. Always use the proper terminology and watch your grammar!

SPELL CHECK! – In any business document that you type and eventually submit or use make sure to always spell check and grammar check. It is definitely not professional or in proper form to have spelling or grammatical errors.
